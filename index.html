I am an incoming Master's student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University, recently graduated in Mechanical Engineering from the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology, specializing in Robotics and Control Systems. My academic interests include scientific machine learning, control theory and multi-agent systems.

At the Technion, I developed Python-based control algorithms for surgical robotics in the Fluids and Elasticity Lab, designed a robotic gripper utilizing neural networks at the Autonomous Robots Lab, optimized autonomous vehicles through the F1tenth initiative, and applied deep learning techniques to learn how to price options, resulting in the preprint "Deep Learning vs. Black-Scholes: Option Pricing Performance on Brazilian Petrobras Stocks".
